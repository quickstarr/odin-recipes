Unicorn Food treats

Unicorns are mythical creatures that need to eat too!.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/8 tsp strawberry extract
  • blue,purple,and pink food coloring

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 175F
  2. Beat egg whites on high until stiff peaks are formed (about 2 minutes)
  3. Add 1/2 cup powdered sugar and beat for another 5 minutes until glossy and stiff.
  4. Gently fold in remaining powdered sugar and the strawberry extract until well combined.
  5. Separate meringue into 3 bowls
  6. Add a few drops of food coloring to each bowl
  7. squeeze the meringue onto a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake at 175 for 2 hours.
  9. let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
  10. ENJOY!
