Lasagna Recipe

One of my favorite dishes is lasagna. Dating back to Ancient Rome and Greece for its simplicity.Often, a quick and easy throw it in the oven and forget meal.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • mushrooms
  • Ground Beef
  • Two pounds of your choice cheese.

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 350F
  2. Brown ground beef in a separate pan
  3. line dish with pasta
  4. add even layer of tomato sauce
  5. add mushrooms
  6. sprinkle cooked ground beef evenly on layer
  7. add cheese
  8. repeat previous steps until dish full
  9. bake for fourty-five minutes.
  10. ENJOY!
