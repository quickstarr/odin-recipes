Sam's Chili

My favorite winter time meal is chili. Let me tell tell you something, ive had my fair share of chili over the years

but nothing compares to the homecooked goodness that Sam creates in her crockpot when it gets nippy outside

Ingredients

  • 2 Pounds Ground meat
  • 1 yellow onion diced
  • 1 larg can diced tomatoes
  • 1 regular can tomato sauce
  • 1 regular can kidney beans
  • 2 gloves garlic
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp cumin

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in a separate pan w/diced onions
  2. add all other ingredients into crockpot
  3. add seasoning
  4. stir and cook on high for 4-5 hours, or low 6-8 hours
  5. ENJOY!
